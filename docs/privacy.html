PanzerVault Bot Privacy Policy

Last updated: 29 April 2026

This Privacy Policy explains what information PanzerVault Bot may collect and how it is used.

1. Information the Bot May Collect

The bot may collect limited Discord-related information required to provide server features, including Discord user IDs, Discord server IDs, role selections, command usage, XP and activity statistics, event RSVP choices, ticket information, gallery or video submission details, onboarding responses, and moderation-related logs.

2. Message Content

The bot may process message content only where needed for enabled features, such as commands, XP, spam detection, tickets, gallery submissions, or moderation support.

Message content is not used to train AI models.

3. How Information Is Used

Information is used to operate bot features, manage roles, provide community tools, support moderation, maintain event and ticket systems, calculate levels, and help server staff manage the Discord server.

4. Data Sharing

The bot does not sell user data. Information may be visible to authorized server staff where needed for moderation, tickets, admin settings, or server management.

5. Data Storage

Bot data may be stored in the bot's database or logs for as long as needed to provide server features, resolve moderation issues, maintain records, or operate the server.

6. Data Removal

Users may contact server staff or the bot owner to request removal of data associated with them, where removal is reasonably possible and not required for moderation, security, or server records.

7. Security

Reasonable steps are taken to protect bot data, but no system can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

8. Third-Party Services

The bot operates on Discord and may depend on Discord APIs and hosting or database services. Use of Discord is also governed by Discord's own Terms of Service and Privacy Policy.

9. Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated when the bot changes, server needs change, or platform requirements change.

10. Contact

For privacy questions or data requests, contact the server staff or bot owner through the Discord server.
